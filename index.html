The Formula

₱1 in.
₱11 back.

For every ₱1 invested, Stuck Market delivers ₱11 of social return to the Philippines. A child who can't read is a ₱66,000 liability. One who can is a ₱66,000 contributor. Together that's ₱132,000 in impact — on a ₱12,000 investment.

SROI = Total Social Value / Total Investment
= ₱132,000 / ₱12,000 = 11x
